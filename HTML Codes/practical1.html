Plot of the Movie

The plot revolves around 4 magicians who each receive a tarot card leading them to New York City apartment, where they discover hologram technology with instructions from an unknown being.

A year later, they all are performing a live magic show under the name "The Four Horseman" in Las Vegas and their final trick blows everyone away, as they teleport a member from the audience inside the vault of The Crédit Républicain Bank in Paris, as stacks upon stacks of euros is showered on top of the the Las Vegas crowd.

And from this point on, their troubles begin, as FBI tries to catch the thief behind all this menace, but to no avail
